Gesturify Commands
Gestures

Play/Pause: Extend hand in a "STOP" motion

Next Song: Close hand and extend thumb left

Previous Song: Close hand and extend thumb right

Fast Forward Song: Rotate the tip of the index finger in a clockwise direction

Rewind Song: Rotate the tip of the index finger in a counterclockwise direction

Voice

Search: Say "search" followed by the type of item you’re searching for. You can search for a "song", "artist", "album", or "playlist". Afterwards say your search query.

Example: Example: "Search song Stay Frosty Royal Milk Tea"

Multimodal (Gestures + Speech)

Volume: Say "change volume" to switch to volume mode. When in volume mode, move your hand vertically with your open hand face down to change the volume. Close your fist or say "set volume" when you’re done.

Add current song to playlist: In the Gesturify user interface, click the Add To Playlist button, point to a playlist and say "Add this song to that playlist".
